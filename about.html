Our Story

Built on Honesty. Driven by Quality.

Pyramid Construction DMV was founded with a simple belief: homeowners in Northern Virginia deserve a contractor they can actually trust. Someone who shows up, explains the work honestly, charges a fair price, and does the job right.

Our team has built a strong reputation across Loudoun County, Fairfax, Prince William County, and the broader Northern Virginia region for transparent communication, quality craftsmanship, and follow-through. We don't upsell work you don't need. We don't disappear after the deposit.

From a full roof replacement to a single window swap, every project gets the same attention and care.
