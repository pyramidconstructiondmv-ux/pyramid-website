The ProVia Endure™ window is what we install most in Northern Virginia homes. It's a premium vinyl double or triple-pane unit engineered to meet the specific thermal and humidity demands of the Mid-Atlantic climate — keeping heat out in July and cold out in January.

Window Styles

Endure™ Window Styles We Install

Double Hung

The most popular style — both sashes tilt in for easy cleaning. Ideal for most Northern Virginia homes.

Casement

Hinged on the side, cranks outward for maximum ventilation and an unobstructed view.

Picture / Fixed

Non-operable for maximum glass area and light. Often paired with casement or double-hung units.

Sliding

Smooth horizontal operation — great for wide openings where vertical space is limited.

Awning

Hinged at top, opens outward — lets in fresh air even during light rain.

Bay & Bow

Adds visual depth and interior space — a popular upgrade for living and dining rooms.

ProVia Eris™ Entry Doors

Entry Doors That Make a Statement

ProVia's Eris™ fiberglass and steel entry doors are custom-built to fit your opening with precision — no filler frames, no exposed gaps. They combine curb appeal with real security: multi-point locking, heavy-duty hinges, and weather stripping that seals out drafts completely.

Fiberglass doors replicate the grain and texture of real wood while requiring zero maintenance — no swelling, warping, or repainting. Steel doors offer maximum security at a lower price point.
