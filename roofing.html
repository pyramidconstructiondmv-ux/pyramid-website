FAQs

Roofing Questions Answered

How do I know if I need replacement vs. repair?

If your roof is under 15 years old and damage is localized, a repair often makes sense. If shingles are curling, granules are in gutters, or your roof is 20+ years old, replacement usually provides better long-term value. We'll tell you honestly which one you actually need.

Do you help with insurance claims?

Yes. We document all damage, provide detailed reports for your insurance adjuster, and can meet with your adjuster on-site. Many roofing jobs in Northern Virginia are fully or partially covered by homeowner's insurance after storm damage.

How long does a roof replacement take?

Most residential roof replacements take 1–2 days. We work efficiently and clean up completely — including magnetic nail sweeps — before we leave.

What areas do you serve for roofing?
