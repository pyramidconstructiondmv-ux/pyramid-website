Fiber Cement — The Best Siding Money Can Buy

James Hardie HardiePlank® is what we recommend for homeowners who want the absolute best. It's engineered fiber cement — meaning it's made from sand, cement, and cellulose fiber — so it handles everything Northern Virginia throws at it: humidity, freeze-thaw cycles, wind, and impact.

HardiePlank installs with the warm, textured look of real painted wood, but it won't rot, warp, crack, or invite insects. ColorPlus® factory-baked paint means the color won't peel or fade for 15+ years with no repainting required.
