Why We Choose MainStreet

The Look of Real Wood — Without the Maintenance

CertainTeed MainStreet® is the vinyl siding we recommend most for whole-home replacements in Northern Virginia. Its deep cedar-grain embossed texture gives homes a natural, upscale look that flat vinyl can't match — and ColorMax® technology means the color stays true for decades without fading, chalking, or peeling.

Unlike wood or fiber cement, MainStreet never needs painting, won't rot, and resists moisture intrusion that drives up energy bills and causes wall damage in our humid summers.
