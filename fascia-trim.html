Warning Signs

Does Your Fascia Need Repair?

Soft or Spongy Wood

If the fascia board feels soft when pressed, it's rotted. This needs replacement before it spreads to the roof deck or rafters above.

Pest Activity

Bees, wasps, or squirrels entering your soffit are a sign of gaps or damage. We seal everything up when we repair.

Gutters Pulling Away

Gutters attach to the fascia board. Rotted fascia causes gutters to sag and pull away — damaging both systems at once.
